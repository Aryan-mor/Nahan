Security Information

NAHAN uses industry-standard OpenPGP encryption with ECC Curve25519 keys.

  • All encryption happens locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Private keys are encrypted with your passphrase
  • Messages are signed and encrypted for authenticity
